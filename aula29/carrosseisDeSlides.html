A walk in the forest

The forest of existence, with mountains of chaos and schizophrenia
These are my abstract and long-winded thoughts.
No organization, aesthetics or intention of any level.

I float like Dadaist poetry in the midst of chaos

I go up, I go down, I disappear and I come back, surrounded by doubts, certainties, paranoia, fears and the standard already eaten away by the glow of belief systems, which are here, there, in the middle and between the lines, in the look, in the voice, at the points, ready to frame.

I wasn't planning anything, without pretense of being good or bad, or believing a concept of morals, I was just a little primate wanting to exist as a nomad, having sex here and there like all the other animals..

Why damn? What more could I want from this existence? delude myself with the same things that everyone deludes themselves?
